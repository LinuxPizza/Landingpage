Do you want your own mailinglists for your product, project or organization?

Get your managed one via LinuxPizza:

The mailinglist is moderated with commands via email instead of a webUI so you can intergrate it much easier with your own software.

You can try subscribing to our demo mailing list by sending an email to demo+subscribe@linux.pizza, please keep in mind that this is a public mailing list for testing only.

To unsubscribe - demo+unsubscribe@linux.pizza
To get a list of commands - demo+help@linux.pizza

If you are interested in our mailinglist-service, feel free to contact me via mastodon or via email.
